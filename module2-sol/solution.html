Chicken

The chicken (Gallus gallus domesticus) is a type of domesticated fowl, a subspecies of the red junglefowl.

Beef

Beef is the culinary name for meat from cattle. Beef can be harvested from bulls, heifers or steers. Its acceptability as a food source varies in different parts of the world.

Sushi

Sushi is a type of food preparation originating in Japan, consisting of cooked vinegared rice (sushi-meshi) combined with other ingredients (neta) such as seafood, meat, vegetables and sometimes tropical fruits.
